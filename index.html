Who are you?

My name's Stephen. Teaching myself through code I find on the web or open sourced, I'm trying to learn web design.

My History

Back in 2016-17, I used to play a game called "Roblox", where you could create and share your own creations in a (somewhat) Blender-like building aspect and a modified Lua scripting enviroment, which has now been changed to "Luau", an updated version. I first learned to code there, again open-sourcing or finding code from interesting games I found, until 2017. At this point I lost interest because of schoolwork and other life events, until 2019-ish, when I found some of my old code and began looking around on GitHub.

You wake up with a jolt.

Why is there a road? Was it always here? Who am I? Where am I? What is this uniform, you ask yourself, finding that you simply do not know. Behind you is wreckage, of what obviously was your past life. Now, you see a long road ahead, and you begin to walk, not knowing why, but having that feeling that you should.

And then it all comes back. Day has broken.
